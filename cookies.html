Cookies

Preparation time: 10 min - Baking time: 9-11 min

Ingredients:

  • Eggs: 2
  • Sugar: 160-170g
  • Melted butter: 170g
  • Flour: 300g
  • Baking powder: 2 teaspoons
  • A pinch of salt
  • Chocolate chips: 200g
  • A pinch of vanilla aroma (optional)

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Mix the eggs, sugar, melted butter (and vanilla aroma) together in a container
  3. Mix the flour, baking powder and salt in another container
  4. Mix the two container together
  5. Add the chocolate chips
  6. Use a spoon to drop large balls of dough on a baking paper and leave enough space between each of them as they'll spread out
  7. Bake for 9-11 min according to the size of the balls
  8. Allow the cookies at least 20 min to cool on the tray to prevent them from breaking

Preparation time: 10 min - Baking time: 10-12 min

Ingredients:

  • Eggs: 2
  • Sugar: 150g
  • Butter (cut in pieces): 125g
  • Flour: 130g
  • Baking powder: 1 teaspoon
  • A pinch of salt
  • Chocolate chips: 200g
  • Unsweetened Cacao powder: 3 tablespoons

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C and prepare all the ingredients
  2. Melt the butter and chocolate together on low heat, stirring constantly to prevent chocolate from burning. Once melted, set aside
  3. Mix the eggs and sugar together for at least 3 min and then add the chocolat-butter mix
  4. Mix the flour, salt, baking powder and cacao together and then add the mix to the chocolate mixture. Mix briefly just to combine
  5. Use a spoon to drop large balls of dough on a baking paper and leave enough space between each of them as they'll spread out
  6. Bake for 10-12 min according to the size of the balls
  7. Allow the cookies at least 20 min to cool on the tray to prevent them from breaking
