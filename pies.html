Pies

Preparation time: 20 min - Baking time: 25-30 min

Ingredients:

  • Puff pastry: 1
  • Eggs: 2
  • Sugar: 100g
  • Melted butter: 100g
  • Flour: 30g
  • Almond powder: 90g
  • Chocolate chips: 100g
  • Pears: 3-4

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C
  2. Mix the eggs, sugar, melted butter together in a container
  3. Mix the flour and almond powder in another container
  4. Mix the two containers together
  5. Add the chocolate chips
  6. Roll out the puff pastry in the tart tin and use a fork to make small holes in it
  7. Remove the skin of the pears, slice them and place them in the tart tin
  8. Add the dough on the pears to evenly recover them
  9. Bake the tart for 25-30 min and wait 15 min before removing it from the tin

Preparation time: 20 min - Baking time: 30 min

Ingredients:

  • Puff pastry: 1
  • Eggs: 2
  • Sugar: 100g
  • Creme fraiche: 25cl
  • Apples: 3-4
  • Vanilla sugar: 10g (optional)
  • Vanilla aroma: (optional)
  • Sliced almond: (optional)

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C
  2. Roll out the puff pastry in the tart tin and use a fork to make small holes in it
  3. Remove the apples skin and slice them. Then place them on the pastry
  4. In a container, mix the sugar(s), the eggs and the vanilla aroma (if you have some)
  5. Then add the creme fraiche to the mixture and keep mixing
  6. Pour evenly the mixture on the apples
  7. Add some sliced almonds on the top if you have some
  8. Bake the tart for 30 min
  9. Allow the tart to cool before removing it from the tin
