Cakes

Preparation time: 35 min - Baking time: 30 min

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable oil: 230ml
  • Natural yogurt: 100g
  • Eggs: 4
  • Vanilla extract: 1.5 teaspoon
  • Zested Orange: 0.5 orange
  • Self-raising flour: 265g
  • Light muscovado sugar: 335g
  • Ground Cinnamon: 2.5 teaspoons
  • Fresh grated nutmeg: 0.25 nutmeg
  • Carrots: 265g (about 3)
  • Raisins: 100g
  • Walnuts or pecan: 100g (optional)
  • Softened butter: 100g
  • Icing sugar: 300g
  • Soft cheese: 100g

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Whisk the oil, yogurt, eggs, vanilla and zest in a jug
  3. Mix the flour, sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg (and a pinch of salt) in a container
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry, along with the carrots, raisins and half the nuts
  5. Mix well and divide between two 20 cm tins
  6. Bake for 25-30 min or until a skewer inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean
  7. The icing: beat the butter and sugar together and add the soft cheese little by little and beat again
  8. Remove the cakes from the tins and sandwich them together with half the icing
  9. Top with the remaining icing and scatter with the remaining walnuts
  10. Then put the cake into the fridge and wait a few hours until the icing gets firm

Preparation time: 20 min - Baking time: 50-60 min

Ingredients:

  • Melted butter: 200g
  • Eggs: 3
  • Sugar: 200g
  • Flour: 200g
  • Baking powder: 8g
  • A pinch ofsalt
  • Chocolate chips: 140g

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Put the chocolate chips in the freezer to prevent them to fall into the bottom of the cake while it's baking
  3. Take the eggs and separate the white from the egg yolk
  4. In a container, mix the sugar and the egg yolks. Then add the melted butter and keep mixing
  5. In another container, mix the flour and the baking powder
  6. In another container, beat the white eggs with a pinch of salt until it gets firm
  7. Then add them carefully to the mixture with a spatula
  8. Remove the chocolate chips from the freezer and add them to the mixture. Stir quicky
  9. Bake the cake for 50-60 min or until a skewer inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean
  10. Allow the cake to cool before removing it from the baking tin
